My Story Why I build software

Originally from Fort Lauderdale, Florida, John moved to the Bay Area three years ago and works on the web services team at Barnes & Noble as a Senior Ruby on Rails Engineer. Prior to Barnes & Noble, John worked for 2.5 years at TCI building a digital K-12 social studies & science subscription service.

John majored in Physics with minors in Economics & History at Washington Unviersity in St. Louis. He has been developing software programs & websites for over 10 years. John's language of choice is Ruby, and he has built applications in Java, JavaScript, & PHP as well.

In his free time, John enjoys skiing, climbing, and cycling.
