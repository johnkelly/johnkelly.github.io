My Story Why I build software

Originally from Fort Lauderdale, Florida, John moved to Silicon Valley five years ago. John works on the Education Services team at Yuzu by Barnes & Noble Education as an Engineering Manager. He has five years of experience working on educational software for K-12 & colleges.

John majored in Physics with minors in Economics & History at Washington University in St. Louis. He has been developing software programs & websites for over 10 years. John's language of choice is Ruby, but has spent considerable amount of time outside of work learning Clojure & Go.

In his free time, John enjoys skiing, golf, climbing, and cycling.
