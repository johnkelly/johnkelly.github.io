My Work The projects I've built

Curt Video (2010) Founder



A social network to share videos of 5 - 45 seconds in length. Curt Video can be thought of as a similar concept to Vine except built 18 months prior to its founding and without the mobile app focus.

Favorite Plate (2011) Founder



A web application that enables people to review restaurants by each menu item instead of as a whole. Favorite Plate can be thought of as a rival to Yelp, but with a food focus instead of a restaurant focus.

TCI (2011 - 2013) Software Engineer



I worked on the TeachTCI, LearnTCI, & TCI Store subscription services. TCI is an educational K-12 software company designing products for history & science teachers and students.

Try Whiteboard (2012) Founder



A subscription as a service web application that allowed business to digitize their company's white boards. Try Whiteboard can be thought of as the online drawing board for meeting rooms.

Zarlu (2013) Founder



A subscription as a service web application that allowed businesses to manage employee time-off requests. Zarlu was a business attendance solution that reduced the work of managing attendance.

Barnes & Noble (2013 - Present) Senior Ruby on Rails Developer


I work on the web services team. A NDA prevents me from giving additional details.

Release[Board] (2013 - Present) Co-Founder



A subscription as a service web application that allows businesses to manage software releases. Release[Board] improves the software release process by improving team communication, organization, and planning.
